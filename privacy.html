Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 27, 2025

Welcome to Lorye Go! Hong Kong Edition. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website hosted at entzyeung.github.io/LoryeGo/. Your privacy matters to us, and we’re committed to being transparent about our practices.

1. Information We Collect

We use Google Analytics (via Google Tag Manager) to collect anonymized data about how visitors use our site. This includes:

  • Page Views: Which pages you visit (e.g., homepage, news articles).
  • Button Clicks: Interactions like downloading the game or clicking "READ MORE" on news items.
  • Device and Browser Info: General data like your device type, browser, and approximate location (e.g., city-level, derived from IP address).

We don’t collect personally identifiable information (e.g., your name or email) unless you explicitly provide it, such as by contacting us.

2. How We Use Your Information

The data we collect helps us:

  • Understand how many people visit and what they enjoy (e.g., which downloads are popular).
  • Improve the site’s design and content based on usage patterns.
  • Troubleshoot issues if something’s not working right.

We don’t sell, trade, or share your data with anyone—it’s just for making Lorye Go! better.

3. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies through Google Analytics to track your activity. These are small files stored on your device that help us analyze site usage. Tracking only starts after you consent via our popup:

  • Consent Popup: On your first visit, we ask you to "Accept" or "Decline" tracking. If you accept, cookies are enabled; if you decline, no tracking occurs.
  • Local Storage: We save your choice in your browser’s local storage so we don’t bug you every time.

You can clear your cookies or local storage anytime to reset your choice.

4. Third-Party Services

We rely on:

  • Google Analytics (GA4): Managed via Google Tag Manager (GTM) to collect and analyze data. Google may process this data per their Privacy Policy.
  • GitHub Pages: Hosts our site. GitHub may log basic access data (e.g., IP addresses) per their Privacy Statement, but we don’t control that.

5. Your Rights

You can:

  • Opt Out: Decline tracking via the popup or clear your browser’s local storage to revoke consent.
  • Ask Questions: Reach out if you’re curious about your data or want it deleted from our analytics (though it’s anonymized).

Since we don’t collect personal details, there’s no account to delete—just stop visiting, and we won’t track you further (if you’ve declined).

6. Contact Us

Questions or concerns? Email me at entzyeung@gmail.com. I’m happy to chat about this policy or the game!

7. Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy (e.g., to add new features or comply with laws), we’ll change the "Last Updated" date above. Check back occasionally!
